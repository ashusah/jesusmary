Play Group

Age: 1.5 – 2.5 years

A warm and gentle introduction to school life. Children learn through play, music, and stories in a safe and loving setting designed to ease the transition from home.

✓
Sensory play and exploration activities
✓
Rhymes, songs, and basic motor skills
✓
Social interaction with peers
✓
2–3 hours daily, flexible timing

Nursery

Age: 2.5 – 3.5 years

Foundational skills in language, early numeracy, creativity, and social interaction — delivered through a rich mix of play-based and guided learning activities.

✓
Introduction to letters and numbers
✓
Arts, crafts, and creative expression
✓
Story time and language development
✓
Outdoor play and group activities

Lower Kindergarten (LKG)

Age: 3.5 – 4.5 years

Structured learning with a focus on early literacy, numeracy, and creativity. Children begin reading readiness, writing practice, and develop stronger social skills.

✓
Phonics and reading readiness
✓
Number concepts up to 20
✓
Drawing, colouring, and craft work
✓
Environmental awareness activities

Upper Kindergarten (UKG)

Age: 4.5 – 5.5 years

Advanced concepts in reading, writing, and numeracy that prepare children confidently for Class 1 entry. Strong focus on independent thinking and communication.

✓
Sentence formation and early writing
✓
Basic addition and subtraction
✓
Hindi and English language exposure
✓
School-readiness skills and routines
