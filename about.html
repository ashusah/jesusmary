Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where every child is empowered to learn at their own pace — guided by the values of Knowledge, Truth, and Faith.

We aim to build not just academic foundations, but character, compassion, and confidence in every young learner.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the most trusted early childhood institution in our community — where families feel welcome, teachers feel inspired, and children feel genuinely at home.

Our learning environment allows each child to nurture the learning minds namely the Empathetic Mind, Conscientious Mind, Focused Mind, Analytical Mind, and Inventive Mind.We envision every graduate of Jesus & Mary's Nursery entering primary school with joy, readiness, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Journey

How We Grew

Founded

Jesus & Mary's Nursery Opens

Started with a single classroom and a vision to provide quality early education rooted in Christian values to families in the community.

Year 3

LKG & UKG Programs Launched

Growing demand from families led us to expand beyond Nursery into structured LKG and UKG programs with trained educators.

Year 7

New Campus & Playground

Moved to a larger premises with a dedicated outdoor playground, activity hall, and separate classrooms for each age group.

Year 10

Activity Club & Summer Camp Introduced

Launched extracurricular programs including music, arts, and summer camp — nurturing talents beyond the classroom.

Today

500+ Families & Growing

Today Jesus & Mary's Nursery is a trusted name in early childhood education, having shaped the foundation of hundreds of young lives.
